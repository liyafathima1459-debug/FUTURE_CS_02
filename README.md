## 📌 Project Overview
Phishing attacks are one of the most common and dangerous cyber threats today,
responsible for over 90% of data breaches worldwide. This project analyzes
real-world phishing email samples, identifies key phishing indicators, and
produces a professional awareness report — helping organizations train employees
and reduce their risk of falling victim to email-based attacks.

---

## ✨ Features
- ✅ Analyzed **4 real-world email samples** for phishing indicators
- ✅ Classified each email as **Safe / Suspicious / Phishing**
- ✅ Identified key indicators like **spoofed domains, urgency tactics, fake links**
- ✅ Explained phishing techniques in **simple, business-friendly language**
- ✅ Provided **clear prevention guidelines** for users and organizations
- ✅ Professionally structured report ready for corporate awareness training

## 🚩 Key Phishing Indicators Covered
- 🔴 **Spoofed sender domains** — fake emails mimicking real companies
- 🔴 **Urgency & fear tactics** — "Act now or lose your account!"
- 🔴 **Suspicious links** — URLs that don't match the claimed website
- 🟡 **Generic greetings** — "Dear User" instead of your real name
- 🟡 **Unexpected attachments** — files you didn't request
- 🟢 **Legitimate indicators** — official domains, personalized content
